Refund & Cancellation

Transparency is our priority.

1. Digital Products

Since Adhyatmik Yatra provides digital products (PDF e-books) that are delivered instantly upon purchase, we generally do not offer refunds once the download link has been generated or accessed.

2. Technical Issues

If you face any issues such as a successful payment but no download link received, please don't worry. Contact us at adhyatmikyatra@zohomail.in with your payment screenshot, and we will manually send you the copy within 24 hours.

3. Payment Failures

In case of a failed transaction where the money was deducted from your account but the order was not confirmed, the refund is automatically processed by your bank or the payment gateway (Razorpay) within 5-7 working days.

4. Cancellation

Orders for digital e-books cannot be cancelled once the payment is processed. For any other support-related queries, feel free to reach out to us via Instagram or Email.
